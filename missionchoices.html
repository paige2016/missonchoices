Mission Choices

Mission Choices is non profit organization that helps people in their time of need.

When Mission Choices started, they payed for everything. Then they got their first donation, which was money. We went around, asking for various donations to keep all the bills paid. A couple of months later, the city, gave us a building because they could not rent or sell it. Slowly, Mission Choices started to recieve grants and various donations ranging from seeds to clothing to other household items.
